Trading In Markets Under Simulation

TiMUS — Your Financial Hub

TiMUS is a zero-risk market simulator that mirrors real trading conditions across stocks, crypto, forex, commodities, and ETFs. Users trade with simulated data and virtual funds to build discipline, learn market mechanics, and explore strategies without financial exposure. The experience blends market education (chart types, risk management, leverage, banking basics) with two trading modes: Classic for deliberate strategy practice and Turbo for fast-paced intraday execution. It is built as an academic project focused on responsible AI use, transparent mechanics, and continuous feedback.
